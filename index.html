Audio Technology

Echo Cancellation. 3D Sound. GSM based on CELP (Code Excited Linear Prediction). Dolby AC-2 based on Modified DCT or MDST. Dolby Digital (AC-3) multichannel coder is a transform coder with AC-2 filterbank at its core. Mpeg-1 Audio has 3 compression methods Audio level I: sub-band coder with a psychoacoustic model (DCT type filter) [1:4 Ratio > 384 kbps] Audio level II: adds more advanced bit allocation techniques and greater accuracy [1:6 to 1:8 Ratio > 256-192 kbps] Audio level III (MP-3): adds a hybrid filterbank and non-uniform quantization plus advanced features like Huffman coding [1:10 to 1:13 Ratio > 128-112 kbps] MPEG-4 Audio: Parametric coding DTS, 5.1, 6.1
